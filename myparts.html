Publication Design
My Parts Are a Problem is a series of publications (Issue 2 is currently in the works) that explores AFAB experiences with their sexual and reproductive organs in society and healthcare systems, through a series of informal interviews. The goal is simply to reduce isolation and shame around these experiences and build empathy and solidarity.
